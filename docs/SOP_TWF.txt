Failure Mode: TWF (Tool Wear Failure)
Diagnostic Overview: Tool wear failure happens when the continuous operational time of the milling tool exceeds its maximum rated lifespan (typically between 200 and 240 minutes, depending on the quality variant).
Troubleshooting Steps: > 1. Safely retract the milling head from the product.
2. Remove the worn tool bit and log its exact usage time in the maintenance database.
3. Install a new tool bit matching the product quality variant (L, M, or H).
4. Reset the tool wear timer in the local telemetry dashboard to 0.
