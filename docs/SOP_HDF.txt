Failure Mode: HDF (Heat Dissipation Failure)
Diagnostic Overview: Triggered when the difference between the air temperature and the process temperature falls below the minimum required threshold for thermal cooling, leading to overheating.
Troubleshooting Steps: > 1. Power down the machine and allow for a 45-minute mandatory cooling period.
2. Inspect the ambient cooling fans and industrial HVAC vents near the machine.
3. Check the machine's internal thermal sensors for dust buildup or recalibration needs.
4. Do not restart until the process temperature drops below 300K.
